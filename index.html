Info Section

  • Remember: before adopting consider whether you can make this commitment to take care of the animal. Do not just adopt on a whim, if you are not planning on taking care of the animal.
  • You can adopt a rescued animal from an animal shelter or an animal rescue group.

Tips Section

  • Rescued animals can be a bit more hesitant and may need some time to warm up to others. Give them some space first, then slowly gain their trust.
  • When you first bring a rescued animal home, do not throw any welcome parties or have visitors come over to your house. The rescued animal needs time to adapt to the new environment and not get overwhelmed by the presence of other people. (source: Animal Rescue League of Boston)
  • Do not give the adopted animal random food, feed them food that the vet or the animal rescue group recommended. (source: Animal Rescue League of Boston)
